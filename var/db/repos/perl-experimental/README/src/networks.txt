[[networks]]
Networks
========

The Gentoo Perl Herd and the overlay contributors have various channels of
communications and interactions.

[[networks-irc]]
IRC
---

The primary source for all direct Gentoo Perl communications is `#gentoo-perl`
on `irc.freenode.org`

[[networks-sourcecontrol]]
Source-Control
--------------

Overlays for Gentoo can be found on xref:repositories-github[Github] and
xref:repositories-ggo[the Gentoo Overlays website].
